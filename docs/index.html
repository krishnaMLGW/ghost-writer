Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Ghost Writer ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Ghost Writer processes the text and images you provide within the app to generate AI-powered replies and roasts. This content is sent to our secure servers and forwarded to AI providers (Anthropic Claude and OpenAI) for processing.

We do not store your messages, screenshots, or generated replies on our servers. All processing is done in real-time and content is not retained after the response is delivered.

Your favorites and history are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

Subscription Information

If you subscribe to Ghost Writer Pro, your purchase is processed through Apple App Store or Google Play Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. We do not have access to your payment details. RevenueCat may collect anonymous usage data as described in their privacy policy.

Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking tools. We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

Third-Party Services

Ghost Writer uses the following third-party services to provide its functionality:

Anthropic (Claude AI) - for generating text responses. Their privacy policy is available at anthropic.com/privacy.

OpenAI (GPT) - for generating text responses. Their privacy policy is available at openai.com/privacy.

RevenueCat - for subscription management. Their privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Data Security

All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS. We do not store user content on our servers.

Children's Privacy

Ghost Writer is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at ghostwriterapp@gmail.com.
